Product introduction 

My QURAN is a free Quran application control for Series of Quran Speaker products.before use this APP, you should have our Quran Speaker with you, and open the APP connect the Speaker through bluetooth, then you can control the speaker.

APP provide the following functions:
1.Playing: to select next/previous Surah or Ayah, or Mp3
and search any Surah or Ayah you want to listen to, and can repeat audio 3 times,select different mode playing for Quran, also can choose the translations,play and pause Audio,turn on/off the light and so on.
2.Reciters: you can select anyone to listen among 35 reciters.
3.LightCtrl: to change 7 colors of the lights
4.Settings:Bluetooth, custom mode, timeing off, Music,language setting, translastions setting,Azan. 


The APP has all the same fucntions as Quran Speakers,can control the speakers without remote control.
